## Logo Usage Rules

- Use `kevixo-logo.svg` on dark product surfaces.
- Use `kevixo-logo-dark.svg` on light backgrounds.
- Use `kevixo-logo-light.svg` only when a white icon tile is needed on a dark background.
- Keep clear space around the mark equal to at least one quarter of the icon width.
- Do not add poker chips, cards, casino colors, shadows, outlines, or decorative symbols.
- Do not stretch, rotate, recolor, or place the mark on low-contrast backgrounds.
- Use the icon alone for square avatars, favicons, and small app surfaces.
